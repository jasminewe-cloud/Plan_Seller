What managing from abroad involves
Finding and vetting a property manager before you leave (allow 3–4 weeks)
Setting up a local maintenance contact for emergencies
Understanding Paris time zone for communication (9 hours ahead of Seattle)
Keeping a reserve fund for unexpected repairs (recommend 3–6 months of costs)
If keeping vacant
Is there someone you trust to house-sit or check in regularly?
Vacant homes still need maintenance, pest control, and periodic walkthroughs
